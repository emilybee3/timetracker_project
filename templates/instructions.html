Thanks for trying Time Mind!



Activity diaries are common components of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy. A crucial information gathering tool, keeping track of your daily activities and moods can give you important insights into your overall wellbeing.

Getting started is super easy! Every day, you'll have the opportunity to fill out our simple activity tracker form. For each hour from 6am to 9pm you can write a simple note about what you were doing and record how you felt during that time: either red (terrible), yellow (neutral), or green (great). We take the rest from there, generating a color coded fully searchable spreadsheet representing your week!

Each day's forms are only available for that day- you can't retroactivly fill out your spreadsheet. This is for a subtle reason backed by research: the longer you wait to fill out your form, the less accurate your report of both your time spent and mood will be.
